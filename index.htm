Art Teaching Philsophy

5.21.2019

Education in the building block of society and the future. Among the building blocks is a special block know as Art. Many think the block is not helpful, but it is there for a reason. I hope to teach for that reason and hope to communicate that reason to the students. All students should go through a full year at least of art to gain the ability of critique thinking and working out ideas. Viewing art as both a way to relax and a way to design anything they put their minds to. Also, to teach them to never shy away from doing something they want to do.

Encouraging the young and give wisdom from the old is what my personal goal is. I was inspired by my own teachers on the way people think. Looking at my Asperger's not as disability, but as in something that is different but positive. Wanting to give what I learned to shine lights onto others. I will always try my best with what I can do to help others and I believe art will be the way to do so.

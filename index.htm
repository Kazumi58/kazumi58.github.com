Art Teaching Philsophy

7.2.2019

Art is a tool that every person should have and be able to use it for different task as needed. Art is created at a young age as an experiment and experience which lights part of the right brain. Going through the drawing stages up to decision on if they want to continue using their skill or not. I believe that they should view the skill a bit more to realize it can do more than just drawing, it can be molded into a skill they can use. Which is the beauty of art, the ability to be molded into something desired. Which art history can show a variety of examples on how art is used.

My role as a future teacher is to share this idea to students and give them an education that can be used more in the real world. I want to ensure that the time used in my classroom will be put to good use for the students in the way of art. For I believe that anyone can do art if they put the mind and effort into learning and creating. Pushing for deeper thinking on the reason why and how to question what they see and not just except it.
